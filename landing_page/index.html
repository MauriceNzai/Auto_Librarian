But why Auto_Librrian? You may wonder...

Some months after joining the program, and as the complexity of the projects therein increased, I opted to visit the local library to find materials for my research. I found a good book that was so helpful in undertaking the projects. One day I decided to borrow this book. To my disappointment, I was told that the book was not available because it has been reserved for the next three months. After three weeks, I received a call from the librarian informing me that there had been a mistake. They hand now found out that there was one more copy available for borrowing. Even though this was a bit late, I was glad that I could have the book at last. From this moment, I decided that I would develop a library management system that would improve the efficiency and one with clear book records stored.
